Why Everyone Should Understand Coding.

September 20, 2016 by Whitney

In our fast-paced technologically based world, having an envolving knowledge of the changing trends is crucial to your individual and professional success. The basic languages of HTML, CSS, and JAVA are somewhat simple to pick up on in a few quiet Saturdays. Websites like CodeAcadmy, W3Schools, and Khan Academy all provide free instruction on basic coding languages.

If your learning style is more hands on, an interactive educational route for coding would be YouTube. You can literally search ANY skill you're interested in learning and find quality step-by-step tutorials that you can hit replay on without feeling dumb. Take a half hour tonight to learn something new, something relevant, and something that will profit your success; learn some code.

Three Benefits of Virtual Reality

September 21, 2016 by Michael

Virtual reality has recently come into the spotlight as a significant jump in technology. It's a pretty mind-blowing concept that a man in rural Oklahoma could walk the streets of Paris simply by putting on a pair of glasses, but what true benefit will virtual reality bring?


  • Convenience. Virtual reality sure beats video conferencing and can make business meetings with remote members way more interactive.

  • Training. Imagine wanting to learn to paint and being able to sit with a highly skilled artist hundreds of miles away. This will be especially great for tactal learners.

  • Mind-blowing entertainment. Put on your virtual reality headset and you're walking the countryside of Skyrim.

While this is still a long way from being a household staple, virtual reality is a trend to keep an eye on.
